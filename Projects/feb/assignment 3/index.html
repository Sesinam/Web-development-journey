VALLEY OF LILIES SCHOOL

It' s a mission-affiliated, preparatory, day school located in Accra, Ghana. The Valley of Lilies School was founded by Ms Ad in 1995. The Ridge Church School is located at Achimota. The school is inter-denominational Christian, holding ties to the Anglican, Methodist and Presbyterian churches in Ghana. The school largely follows the prescribed curriculum and syllabus of the Ghana Education Service (GES).
School Mission
The overarching mission of the school includes development; systematic and consistent learning; imparting honesty and courtesy; loyalty, harmony, love and compassion; dedication and discipline. The specific objectives of the school are as follows: "Promotion of systematic and consistent learning habits
Encouragement and reward of hard work and good stewardship
Teaching and imparting of honesty, courtesy, justice and fair play
Development of a spirit of writing, loyalty, harmony, love and compassion
Witnessing by life, deed and word, the Gospel of Jesus Christ
Raising disciplined, dedicated, God-fearing and morally upright citizens for Church and Nation"
Facilities and Infrastructure
Apart classrooms, the school has a multi-purpose block that houses a library, an assembly hall, computer laboratory, washrooms, an assembly hall, washrooms and a storeroom. Other blocks have a kindergarten and a playroom, staff rooms, a school cafeteria and a canopy walkway connecting the JHS block to the kindergarten department. The school also owns a home economics centre, an administration block a science laboratory, an infirmary and a pre-technical skills workshop for wood and metalwork. There is also a basketball court and a football field on the campus.
